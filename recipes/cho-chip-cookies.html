Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are very tasteful chocolate chip cookies. They are very easy to prepare, you can even ask your kiddos to help you bake. You can find all of the ingredients at your local grocery store in the baking goods aisle.

Preparation and Serving Size

  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Additional Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes
  • Servings: 24 people
  • Yield: 4 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon backing soda
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)

Step 2:

Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 3:

Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
