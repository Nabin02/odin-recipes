Vegetable Rice

Crunchy delicious salad you can prepare on your break from your work from home job. Easy to prepare that only takes 15 minutes and can be ready to eat in 15 minutes. This recipie has a serving size of 12 cups.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg (22 oz) Wegmans Fresh Romaine Hearts, cored, quartered lengthwise, cut in bite-size pieces
  • 3 Tbsp Italian Classics Shredded Parmigiano Reggiano (Cheese Shop)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Wegmans Amore Caesar Dressing
  • 1 pkg (5 oz) Wegmans Caesar Twice-Baked Croutons

Directions

  • Combine lettuce and cheese in large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Add dressing and croutons; toss to combine.
