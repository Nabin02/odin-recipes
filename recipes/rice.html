Vegetable Rice

Very simple vegetable rice recipe for you to make for your work lunch or for your kids to take to school for lunch. Easy to heat and healthy option. Takes 20 minutes to prepare and cook. Serves four people (adults).

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Oil
  • 1 Medium Sized Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Large Carrot, Chopped
  • 1 Celery Rib, Chopped
  • 1/2 Red Pepper, Chopped
  • 14-1/2 Ounce Chicken Broth
  • 1-1/2 Cups White Rice, Uncooked
  • 1 Cup Frozen Peas
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Directions

  • Heat oil in a skillet.
  • Add onion, carrot, celery, and red pepper; cook 5 minutes or until tender.
  • Add broth; bring to a boil.
  • Stir in rice and peas; cover.
  • Simmer 5 minutes.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
